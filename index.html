Our story

Children outgrow everything.
The products don't go anywhere.

A bicycle bought for ₹8,000 is ridden for 14 months — then parked in a storeroom forever. Football boots. Cricket kits. Roller blades. School shoes worn twice. All bought with enthusiasm, outgrown with inevitability, discarded with guilt.

Less than 4% of used sporting goods in India are resold or recirculated through any organised channel. Billions in quality assets gather dust in Indian homes every year.

PLAYeBACK™ exists to flip this story. We built a managed active lifecycle platform — every asset is collected, cared for, certified with a full SafeScore™ and redistributed to its next family, academy or community.

No strangers. No haggling. No anxiety. Play. Pause. Replay.

"Every product deserves another season."
