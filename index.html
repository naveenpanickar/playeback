Our story

Children outgrow everything.
The products don't go anywhere.

A bicycle bought for ₹8,000 is ridden for 14 months — then parked in a storeroom forever. A cricket kit. Roller blades. Football boots. All bought with enthusiasm, outgrown with inevitability, and discarded with guilt.

Less than 4% of used sporting goods in India are resold through any organised channel. Billions of rupees of quality gear is gathering dust in Indian homes right now.

PLAYeBACK exists to flip this story. We built a managed circular loop — every product is collected, refurbished, certified with its full history, and redistributed to the next family at a fraction of the new price.

No strangers. No haggling. No anxiety. Just trust, built product by product — and a credit in your wallet for every item you hand back.

"Every product deserves another season."
